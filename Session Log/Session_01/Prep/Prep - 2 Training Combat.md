# Pre-Combat Briefing 
**Scene framing**
- Players enter individual training pods.
- Interior lights narrow to a slit.
- A brief hiss as seals engage
- After a pause, light floods in as the pod resolves into a familiar mech cockpit.
- COMP/CON HUDs boot into a simulation environment.
**Kiora (over comms):**
> “I’ll be providing your mission briefing in lieu of a dedicated officer.”

> “This scenario is reconstructed from a historical engagement. A full briefing packet has been forwarded to your COMP/CONs, but the situation should be straightforward.”

> “You will be acting as Union forces deployed during the Dawnline Shore conflict. Enemy frames are simulated Harrison Armory assets.”

> “A VIP transport has crash-landed in the area. Your objective is to extract the VIP. Enemy units consider the VIP a priority target but will not deliberately harm them.”

> “Enemy reinforcements are expected from the northeast—marked in red. The VIP and extraction zone are marked in orange.”

> “Once the VIP is freed, you are to secure the landing zone for two full rounds.”

> “You should now have a tactical overview. Zones of soft cover are marked in green; hard cover in pale red.”

> “Questions?”
### Turn Structure (Early, Before First Activation)
- Explain **Player Go / Enemy Go** turn flow.
- You can push through woods, but it'll slow you
- Explain **action economy**:
    - **Two Quick Actions**  
        (e.g. Skirmish, Tech, Boost)
    - **OR One Full Action**  
        (e.g. Barrage, Full Tech, Stabilize)
     - **One Reaction per Turn**
	     - Start with Overwatch and brace 
	     - Overwatch blast someone in the threat of your mounts.
	     - Brace (resistance to damage and -1 accurarcy till end of turn. No more reactions and only one quick actopm)
	     - These reset on round start
- Emphasize: _You choose each turn; there is no preset rotation._

---

### Early Engagement (First Contact)
- When players first engage enemies:
    - Casually note that some enemy frames look unfamiliar or specialized.
    - Suggest it _might_ be worth scanning for intel.
- If a player asks:
    - Explain **Scan** rules.
    - Give immediately useful information (role, threat, or reinforcement hint).

---

### Early–Mid Combat (1–2 Turns In)
- Introduce **Overcharge**:
    - Grants an additional **Quick Action**
    - Costs **Heat**
    - Risk vs reward framing

---

### Mid Combat Concepts
- Mention **Core Powers**:
    - They are powerful
    - Limited use
    - Not required to use in this fight
- Frame them as “tools you’ll want to remember exist.”

---
### Reinforcements
- Trigger reinforcements at the planned moment.
- Deliver with a clear cue (e.g. Kiora over comms).
---
### Objective Phase (VIP Freed)
- State clearly:
    - **Hold the landing zone for two full rounds**
- Track this out loud.
## End of Combat
- Once conditions are met, fade the simulation:
    - HUD elements dim
    - Hostile markers drop out
    - Pod lighting returns
