## Arival at Reihenstadt — Scene Framing

The **UNS-LS _Last Refuge of Fools_** has reached its destination: **Reihenstadt**.

You’re gathered in a wide docking bay, mag-boots disengaged. There’s **no gravity** but there's plenty of handholds, guidance lights, and a pleasant hum. A boarding tube from the orbital station will be connecting shortly.

You’ve been told you’ll be meeting a local operative assigned to work with you.

**Have players describe their chars, and give a sec to chat.**

Nearby, a familiar hologram resolves into view: **Kiora**, the ship’s NHP.

---

### **Kiora (over comms):**

> “Alright, everybody. I know you’re all eager to stretch your legs after a stint in transit, but try to stay put for the new arrival.”

> “He’s a local. Assigned to work with you. So play nice and don’t scare him off.”

> “You’ll have a training session to get used to each other. After that, some R&R. Then a final briefing w/ the captain. Then you’re out of here.”

> "Well, me too. You won't be free of me that easy."

The hologram flickers slightly as Kiora folds her arms.

---

There’s a sharp **hiss** as the boarding tube makes contact, followed by a faint **jolt** that ripples through the bay.

Warning lights blink green.

The tube iris opens.

---

## Introduction

The new arrival floats through from the station side, guided by the tube’s internal rails.

**Have player introduce themself.**

Let the party react and exchange a few words.

---

### **Kiora:**

> “Good. Everyone’s here.”

> “Follow the illuminated corridors. Training suite is already spun up.”

As she speaks, **path lights ignite** along the bulkheads, forming a clear route deeper into the ship.
