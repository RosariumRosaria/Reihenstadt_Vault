### Scene Transition
The simulation dissolves. HUD elements fade, weapons power down, and the training pod seals disengage with a soft hiss.

**Kiora (over comms):**
> “Well done, everyone. Almost as good as we did in the field.”  
> “You’ve got your Union-mandated break. Feel free to catch up on sleep, get some food, or do whatever it is you meat bags do.”  
> “The Captain wants a debrief in three hours. Docking Bay 16. Don’t be late.”

The channel closes.
You have a few hours to yourselves.

### Downtime – Gus’ Farewell

As the Lancers regroup before heading to Bay 16, Gus intercepts them in the corridor.

He’s carrying four brown paper bags.

> “Ahh Charlie! It’s been a pleasure having you. Bribed OPSEC with a cake to get your calendar. Heard you’re leaving — gonna miss you.”

He offers the bags. They’re warm. Carefully packed. Meant to last.

> “In case the food isn’t up to snuff down there.”

The bags contain proper meals — not ration bricks. Something that tastes like home.

Gus grins.

> “Maybe I’ll have that restaurant planetside some day.”

> “When you’re all famous, I’ll pin your portraits on the wall! Good for business.”

He laughs heartily, then hesitates.

A beat.

Softer:

> “Could I maybe get a picture of the four of you?”
