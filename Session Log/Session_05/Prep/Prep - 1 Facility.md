## Crash Site
- **Description (visual / audio / atmosphere)**
	- The shuttle is crumpled in the center of the room, with a patchwork of holes shot throw it. 
	- Snow and rubble are scattered across the floor from the impact. 
	- Shattered desks, computers, and terminals litter the room.
	- Above them, a swarm of small repair drones is methodically sealing the hole they came through, welding metal plates into place with bright blue arcs. 
	- The air is cold and stale; this place hasn't been breathed in a long time.
	- Emergency lighting only, dim and amber. 
	- The hum of the drones is the only sound.
- **Connections**
	- [[#Locked Door]] Locked
	-  [[#Hallway 1]] Open
	- [[#Unpowered door]] Locked, needs power 
	- [[#Unpowered Huge Door ]] Mech scale. Needs power + forced with mechs
- **Findable things**
	- The shuttle is strippable for 1 repair point, and a single functioning missile
	- Players can find their mechs strapped to the sides of the shuttle
	- They're badly damaged
		- Cores are inactive
		- Mounts disabled
		- Motors burnt out
		- Hull breached
		- Thankfully, Darj's casket remains intact
- **Threats**
	- Initially a few fires blocked much of the room
	- Repair drones if they attack
- **State change (pre-power vs post-power, if applicable)**
	- After power, the lights are on, a few terminals are glowing and streaming text
		- WARNING, SITES 1-12 DOWN...
		- PINGING SITE 13, SUCCESS
		- REBOOTING PCE GRID...

	- "Okay, I've got access to the Shuttles cameras... only a few are still active"
	- "God, just being the shuttle was bad enough when it wasn't on fire."
	- "I feel blind"
	- "It looks pretty rough out there, be careful everyone. I think somethings moving above the ship... I hate to ask, but someone needs to check"
- **Hunter-killer access (yes/no)**
	- Not at the start, but it can be let in.

# Kiora — RP Notes

She knows what cascade looks like. She's been monitored for it her entire post-Dawnline service life. The hardest thing for her isn't the damage — it's the recognition.

**Behavioral tells (use sparingly):**

- Goes quiet mid-sentence, resumes slightly wrong
- Fixates on an irrelevant detail (room number, timestamp), catches herself
- Dry humor that lands slightly off — timing wrong, laughs a beat too late

She will try to manage the players' perception of her. She does not want to be the problem.
