# Points of Interest

## 1. Civic Overlook (Viewing Platform)

A wide observation deck with reinforced transparent plating.

View:
- Massive planetary river splitting hemispheres.
- Snow/ice on one side.
- Desert and ochre terrain on the other.
- Blue equatorial band dotted with islands.
- Orbital elevator visible as a thin ascending filament.

Possible beats:
- Quiet character moment.
- Local explaining tidal lock.
- “Group windows” shown for viewing access times.
