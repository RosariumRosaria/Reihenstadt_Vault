# Staging

This folder is for wiki content you're drafting but haven't published yet.

Use it for:
- Debriefing reports before they go on the wiki
- Inbox messages you're writing
- New lore pages in draft
- Anything that's on its way from "GM secret" to "player-facing"

When something is published to the wiki, either delete it from here or move it to an `_archive` subfolder.
