# Heimkrone (Political Entity)

## Overview
- **What they are:** The local governing body of [[Reihenstadt]], based on [[Heimkrone station]]
- **What they claim to do:** Manage the planet's affairs, represent its people, facilitate reintegration
- **Reality:** 400 years of isolation created a self-sufficient political culture that doesn't necessarily want Union oversight. Kept isolated from the effects of [[MUNDLIFARI]], hence their separation from the ground

## Culture
- Bureaucratic, structured, orderly
- [[Kalenderkarte]] system as social infrastructure
- Civic pride in self-sufficiency
- SecComm heritage acknowledged but reframed as "stewardship"

## Political Position
- Negotiating reintegration with [[Union]], but from a position of "we managed fine without you"
	- Originally excited to return, but this ended when they realized they'd changed from the good old days
- [[HA]] has economic leverage — a Heimkrone/HA partnership is plausible
- If von Coln isn't found, Heimkrone will use his absence to justify distancing from Union
