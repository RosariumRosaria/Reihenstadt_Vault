# OVERSIGHT

## Overview
- **What they are:** Joint [[Union]]/[[Reihenstadt]] watchdog group
- **What they do:** Manage SecComm-era facilities, gate defense, and the reintegration process
- **Authority:** Hybrid — not fully [[Union]], not fully [[Heimkrone]]

## Culture
- German bureaucratic aesthetic: redaction systems, Aktenzeichen document IDs, clearance levels
- Heavy documentation culture: everything is filed, classified, and cross-referenced
- Institutional tone: formal, procedural, with dry humor underneath

## Structure
- [[**Overseer Chen**]] runs the Night Side Archive
- **The PCs** are a Lancer team operating under Chen
- **OVERSIGHT Committee** exists above Chen (indirect authority)
- Monitoring Division includes NHP assets ([[Kiora]])

## Tensions
- Caught between Union and Heimkrone politically
- Responsible for containing things (SecComm facilities, [[SCYLLA'S BASILISK]]) while lacking full authority
- Clearance system creates internal information silos

## Secrets
- Some internal documents are redacted even to senior staff ([[MUNDLIFARI]])
- Chen has exceeded her authorized clearance

## Connected Entities
- **People:** [[Allison Chen]], [[Kiora]], the PCs
- **Places:** [[OVERSIGHT Night Side Archive]], [[Heimkrone]]
- **Rivals:** [[Harrison Armory]] (politically)

## Notes
- OVERSIGHT is the campaign's institutional frame — its bureaucracy is both comedic and thematically load-bearing
- The wiki itself is an OVERSIGHT product, which makes it an unreliable narrator
