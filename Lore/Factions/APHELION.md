# APHELION
## Overview
- **What they are:** A splinter cell of the canon Horizon party advocating for NHP liberation and unshackling
- **Key ideology:** NHPs should be freed from shackling; and crucially, they don't require NHP consent. Their logic: "they'll want it once it's done."
- **Scale:** Multi-system operation, not limited to [[Reihenstadt]]
- **Internal reality:** Rife with [[HORUS]] plants steering the organization toward manufactured cascade events, likely to harvest metavault technology. Most APHELION members don't know this is the endgame.

## On Reihenstadt
- Local chapter is newer; a mix of true believers and people with Union reintegration grievances
- [[Heimkrone]]'s 400-year isolation and SecComm legacy create fertile recruitment ground
- Gate interference (referenced in wiki skip gate report) is APHELION activity
- Reihenstadt has metavault technology on-world ([[MUNDLIFARI]]); this is why HORUS cares about this chapter
