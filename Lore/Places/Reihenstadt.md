# Reihenstadt

## Overview
- Tidally locked planet, recently recontacted after ~400 years of isolation
- Skip gate *[[Alpspitze]]* failed during SecComm era; repaired with help from [[UNS-EX Icebox]]
- Reintegration with [[Union]] is ongoing and politically tense
- [[Harrison Armory]] has substantial local holdings

## Geography
- **Day Side:** Northern desert, extreme heat
- **Morning Side:** Agricultural zones, farm-to-plate culture
- **Equatorial Band:** New Danube river system, island corridors, Capital Cordon
- **Evening Side:** Nightlife district, the Spire
- **Night Side:** Frontier, ski slopes, permanent dark; OVERSIGHT Archive is here
- Orbital elevator connects surface to [[Heimkrone Station]]

## Culture
- [[Kalenderkarte]] system: assigned waking hours, work periods, leisure windows
- Color-coded groups (Green, Blue, Grey, etc.)
- Deep bureaucratic culture — adapted to life without a day-night cycle
- Founded 4390u by settlers from the "Old Danube"

## Political Situation
- Union administrator [[Franz von Coln]] is missing
- [[Harrison Armory]] has local economic power
- Diplomatic summit approaching — outcome determines Union vs HA influence
- OVERSIGHT is the joint watchdog managing the transition
