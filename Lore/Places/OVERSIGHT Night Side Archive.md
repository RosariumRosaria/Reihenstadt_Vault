# OVERSIGHT Night Side Archive

## Overview
- A modest [[OVERSIGHT]] archival and research facility on the Night Side
- Built atop the ruins of a much larger SecComm-era installation
- Just barely on the night side — permanent twilight
- A train stops nearby in a small town

## Atmosphere
- **First impression:** Quiet, controlled, smaller than expected
- **Sounds/smells:** Low ambient hum, recycled air, old concrete beneath newer panels
- **Behavior:** Soft voices, careful movement, frequent checks of logs and readouts

## What's Normal Here
- Filing, cross-referencing, passive monitoring, long hours
- **Draws attention:** Unscheduled access requests, unexplained data gaps
- **People pretend not to notice:** Sealed doors, legacy infrastructure, unexplained alerts that resolve themselves

## Secrets
- Portions of the underlying SecComm facility were never fully mapped or decommissioned
- Some archived material is kept on-site because moving it would raise more questions than leaving it buried
- The building feels smaller than the footprint it sits on
- New construction avoids certain subsurface levels without explanation
- Officially a dead end for records; in practice, where unresolved issues go to wait
