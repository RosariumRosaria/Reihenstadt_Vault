# Heimkrone

## Overview
- [[Reihenstadt]]'s orbital station and primary port of entry
- Seat of planetary leadership — the "upper capital"
- Connected to the surface by orbital elevator
- Spinning ring design with multiple levels; lowermost ring is the public promenade

## Atmosphere
- Clean, structured, civic architecture
- Controlled lighting, neutral tones
- Bustling but orderly — vendors, families, workers in transit

## Known Areas
- **Promenade (Lowermost Ring):** Shops, food stalls, viewing platform. Tourist and local mix. Color-coded signage tied to Kalenderkarte groups.
- **Civic Overlook:** Wide observation deck — view of the planet, the river, the hemispheres, the orbital elevator.
- **Market District:** Precision metalwork, textiles, winter gear. Vendors polite but reserved. Grey status gets checked.
- **SecComm Heritage Exhibit:** Off the main promenade. Old uniforms, deactivated drones, frontier stabilization footage. References "stewardship" and "Containment Lattices." Some sections "under restoration."
- **Integration Office:** Small, windowless. Where Albert works.
- **Upper Levels:** Restricted. Leadership works here.

## Secrets
- The Heritage Exhibit mentions underground defense installations — this is real, not just historical
- Kept isolated from the effects of [[MUNDILIFARI]]
