# Albert

## Overview
- **Affiliation:** [[Heimkrone Station]] bureaucracy
- **Role:** Integration interviewer — processes incoming personnel
- **Current Posting:** Small windowless interview room on Heimkrone

## Personality
- Old, methodical, unflappable
- Processes intake verbally instead of paper, then reruns it for triplicate compliance
- Works in reverse alphabetical order and cites statutes by number
- Deadpan delivery; "the interview was only a formality"
- References "higher powers" having already approved the team
